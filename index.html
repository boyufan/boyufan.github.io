Biography

I am currently a second year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science, Faculty of Science, University of Helsinki, supervised by Prof.Pan Hui. Previously, I received the bachelor degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology in Taiyuan University of Technology in 2017 and the master degree from Beihang University in 2020. My research interests include Pervasive Computing, Federated Learning and Machine Learning.
